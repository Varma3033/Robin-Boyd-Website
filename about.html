ABOUT US

Created by an independent student at the University of Canberra, this website explores the life and highlights some of the key projects of Robin Boyd, a leading architect and critic in Australia, known for his modernist approach. Furthermore, this site seeks to educate viewers and celebrate his dedication to innovation and simplicity, showcasing his key ideology that “good design can improve people’s lives and our world.”
